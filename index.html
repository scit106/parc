The PARC

Performing Arts Rehearsal Center

About

PARC is dedicated to providing affordable rehearsal space for performing arts groups, including theater, dance, and music. We are currently looking to open a space in Cambridge, MA.

Rates & Booking

Our goal is to offer space rental rates as low as $15 an hour along with easy, automatic availability checking and instant booking.

Want to help?

We're still looking for spaces, and there will be a lot of work to be done when we find one. Fill out this form if you want to be kept in the loop or offer your ideas!
